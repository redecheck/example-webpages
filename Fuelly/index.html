Privacy Policy

Fuelly is committed to respecting your privacy.
We will never sell or trade your registration information or spam you (you can turn off any/all email notifications in your user settings). Fuelly will not share such information with partners, affiliates, vendors, advertisers, sponsors or other third parties, unless required by law.
We do not monitor the IP address of those who visit our site, except in aggregate totals so that we know how many people are visiting. When you log in, we set a cookie that identifies you during your session (so that you don't need to log in every time you visit). This cookie will expire when you log out.
Remember that any and all information added to the site is public, so be mindful of how much information you want to share online. Your fuel data is public and shared with other drivers and visitors to the site.
